After School

Holiday Programmes

Keep busy these school holidays with the Amazon and Element School holiday programme. Skaters are aged between 4 and 14 and all levels are welcome.

Beginners & Intermediate Skateboarding

Hang with our pro skateboard instructors and learn the basic first steps to skateboarding while learning how to have fun at a skate park with your friends. Our instructors will cover balance, stance, safety, grinds, ollies and more! All levels are welcome from absolute beginners to those who can do cool tricks already! Board and helmet compulsory. Cool Amazon and Element prizes to be won!

Skate Park Tours

Hang with local pro skaters including head instructor Simon Thorp and spend the day skating at many of Auckland's coolest skate parks! This is an action packed day out for skaters who enjoy rolling around a skate park. All skaters receive an Element hat or t-shirt at the end of the tour.
